About Us

Established in the year 1995 at Madurai, Tamil Nadu, we “Sri Velavan Traders Private Limited” are a “Private Limited” based firm, engaged as the Distributor, Trader of Sika Products, Anti Termite Chemicals, Fosroc Products, Pest Control Products. Leveraging the skills of our qualified team of professionals, we are instrumental in offering a wide range of these products. Our products are known for their utmost quality and reasonable price. Sri Velavan Traders Private Limited is one of the leading construction chemicals & building materials distributor, trader in South India, serving Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Andhra and Telangana. For our accomplishment story, we are grateful to our Mentor T Chellapandi, whose continual backing and direction have been useful to us for attaining exponential development in the current market.
